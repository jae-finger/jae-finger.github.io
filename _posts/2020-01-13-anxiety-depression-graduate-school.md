Maybe not every college and not a complete secret, but from what the doctor told me, it is most graduate schools.
“It’s nothing to be ashamed of,’’ she said. “Probably half the students are on medication like this. If it’s not for depression then anxiety — or both.”
If my doctor was right, then every other graduate student has needed help for anxiety and depression. Could this be true? Is the person on my left but not my right is in a similar struggle?
I figured the doctor was exaggerating to console me. Because if so many students are struggling, why is no one talking about it?
